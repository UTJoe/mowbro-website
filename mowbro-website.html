Built for the field

Everything you need to
run your business.

Built for operators who work outside with gloves on. Big buttons, no fluff, zero confusion.

👥

Homie Management

Store your customers' info, service preferences, and flat rates in one place. Know exactly who's scheduled, who's waiting, and who's overdue.

📅

Smart Scheduling

Set weekly or biweekly recurring cuts. Cancel a single cut without touching the recurring schedule. One-time stops and reschedule queue — all handled.

🗺

Daily Route View

See all your stops for today in one list. Tap to navigate in Apple Maps. Mark cuts complete and move on.

💰

Earnings Tracking

Track what you've earned today and this week without a spreadsheet. See your full cut history per customer at a glance.

🌤️

Weather at a Glance

Know before you load up the truck. Real-time weather with straight-talk labels — "Perfect day to get it done" or "Stay Home, Bro."

🔄

Reschedule Queue

Rained out? Cancellations go straight into your queue. One Homie, one slot. Pick a new date and you're sorted.

Simple by design

Up and running in
under 5 minutes.

No onboarding call. No tutorial video. Just download and go.

1

Create Your Account

Sign in with Apple or Google. Set your service area, work schedule, and the services you offer.

2

Add Your Homies

Enter your customers — name, address, rate, and how often they need a cut. That's it.

3

Start Your Route

Every morning, open the app and see exactly who you're cutting today. Navigate stop by stop.

4

Mark It Done

Check off each cut, track your earnings, and wrap up the day knowing exactly where you stand.
